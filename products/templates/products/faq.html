Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

Ordering & Shipping

How long does shipping take?
Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days within the continental US. Express shipping (1-2 days) is available for an additional fee. International shipping varies by location.

Do you ship internationally?
Yes! We ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination. Please contact us for specific rates to your location.

What if my order is damaged during shipping?
We'll replace any damaged items at no cost to you. Please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the damage, and we'll send replacements immediately.

Products & Ingredients

How should I store my hops and yeast?
Hops should be stored in the freezer in their original packaging or vacuum-sealed bags. Liquid yeast should be refrigerated, while dry yeast can be stored in the refrigerator or freezer.

What's the shelf life of your ingredients?
Hops: 2-3 years when properly stored frozen. Liquid yeast: 4-6 months refrigerated. Dry yeast: 2+ years when stored properly. Malt: 1-2 years in cool, dry conditions.

Do you offer bulk discounts?
Yes! We offer volume discounts for orders over 10 pounds of hops or 50 pounds of malt. Contact us for custom pricing on large orders.

Brewing Support

Can you help me create a recipe?
Absolutely! Our brewing team loves helping customers develop recipes. Email us at brewing@hopandbarley.com with your style preferences and we'll work with you to create the perfect recipe.

What if I'm not happy with my beer?
We want you to love your homebrew! If you're not satisfied with the results, contact us and we'll help troubleshoot the issue and provide guidance for your next batch.

Do you offer brewing classes?
Yes! We regularly host brewing workshops and classes. Check our Community page for upcoming events, or contact us to schedule a private session.

Account & Returns

How do I create an account?
Click the "Register" button in the top right corner and fill out the simple registration form. You'll be able to track orders, save recipes, and access exclusive content.

What's your return policy?
We accept returns within 30 days of purchase for unopened products. Opened ingredients cannot be returned for safety reasons, but we'll work with you to resolve any issues.

How do I track my order?
Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also log into your account to view order status and tracking information.
